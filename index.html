Delhi Public School, Noida is a 5-Pvt. Unaided 3-Co-educational school in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in 1982 by the DPS Society. Delhi Public School, Noida has affiliation with the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. The motto of the school is Service Before Self.
